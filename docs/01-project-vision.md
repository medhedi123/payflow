# PayFlow — Project Vision

## 1. Product Summary

PayFlow is a fintech SaaS platform for businesses and customers.

It combines:

- Business invoicing
- Payment links
- QR code payments
- Customer management
- Digital wallet payments
- Transaction tracking
- Reports and notifications

The goal is to help small and medium businesses create invoices, request payments, track payment status, and receive money through a wallet-based payment flow.

## 2. Problem

Many small businesses manage payments manually using phone calls, messages, screenshots, paper invoices, and spreadsheets.

This creates problems:

- Difficult payment tracking
- No centralized invoice history
- Manual customer follow-up
- No simple QR/payment link flow
- Limited reporting
- Poor visibility over paid and unpaid invoices

## 3. Solution

PayFlow gives businesses a simple dashboard to:

- Create invoices
- Generate payment links
- Generate QR codes
- Track payment status
- Manage customers
- Export reports
- Receive notifications

Customers can:

- Use a digital wallet
- Pay invoices
- Send and receive money
- View transaction history

## 4. Target Users

### Merchants

Examples:

- Travel agencies
- Gyms
- Restaurants
- Freelancers
- Consultants
- Small shops
- Service companies

### Customers

People who receive payment links or invoices and pay using their PayFlow wallet.

### Admins

Platform administrators who manage users, merchants, KYC requests, transactions, and platform activity.

## 5. Core Value

PayFlow is not only a wallet.

It is a business payment platform where the wallet is the payment engine behind invoices, QR payments, and payment links.
